Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices of bacon
  • 8 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 8 slices of bread

Instructions:

1. Cook the bacon in a skillet until crispy. Remove and set aside.

2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.

3. Place a slice of cheese on the non-buttered side of 4 slices of bread.

4. Top with bacon and tomato slices.

5. Add another slice of cheese on top of the tomato.

6. Place the remaining slices of bread on top, buttered side out.

7. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Cook the sandwiches until golden brown on both sides and the cheese is melted.

8. Serve hot and enjoy!
